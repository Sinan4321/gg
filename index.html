Top 10 Food Spots in Calicut

1. Paragon Restaurant

Established in 1939, Paragon is renowned for its authentic Malabar cuisine, especially the flavorful biryanis and seafood dishes.

Address: Kannur Road, Calicut

Website: www.paragonrestaurant.net

2. Sagar Hotel

Famous for its spicy Kerala-style seafood and delicious meals.

Address: Mavoor Road, Calicut

3. Rahmath Hotel

Known for its legendary Calicut Biriyani, a must-try for biryani lovers.

Address: S.M. Street, Calicut
